Newest Artists

Alina Lee - Contemporary Sculpture

Alina Lee's sculptures are a masterful interplay of form and space, where minimalist design meets thought-provoking concepts. Her work often explores themes of identity, transformation, and the relationship between humans and their environments. With a focus on clean lines and geometric shapes, Alina’s sculptures invite quiet reflection and dialogue.

Jessica Carter - Abstract Expressionism

Jessica Carter’s work is a celebration of movement and colour. Her abstract paintings, filled with bold brushstrokes and dynamic forms, evoke raw emotion and energy. Inspired by the fluidity of nature and the human experience, Emily’s pieces offer viewers a journey through vibrant landscapes of the imagination.

Liam Bennett – Visionary Photography

Liam Bennett is a visionary photographer whose work transcends traditional boundaries of photography, blurring the lines between reality and imagination. Known for his ethereal landscapes and striking portraits, Liam’s photos evoke a dreamlike quality, where light, shadow, and texture are masterfully manipulated to create surreal, otherworldly scenes. His work often explores themes of time, memory, and the unseen connections between humans and the natural world.
